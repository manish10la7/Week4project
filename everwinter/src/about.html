Ever Winter

Winter is the coldest season of the year in polar and temperate zones. It occurs after autumn and before spring in each year.

Characteristics

Winter is often associated with snow, ice, and cold temperatures. It is a time when many animals hibernate, and plants enter a dormant state.

  • Shorter days and longer nights.
  • Snowfall in many regions.
  • Freezing temperatures in colder climates.

Activities

Winter offers a variety of activities that people enjoy:

  • Skiing and snowboarding.
  • Building snowmen and having snowball fights.
  • Drinking hot chocolate by the fire.

Did You Know?

  • The winter solstice marks the shortest day of the year.
  • Antarctica experiences winter for six months straight.
  • Snowflakes are unique and no two are exactly alike.
